How It Works

Four steps from ICP to
qualified conversation.

A structured process that runs continuously — building, enriching, scoring, and outreaching while your team focuses on closing.

01 🎯
ICP Definition

We work with you to define your Ideal Customer Profile precisely: industry, role, company size, revenue, geography, tech stack, hiring signals, funding stage. The tighter the ICP, the better the results.

02 🔍
AI Prospect Research

AI scans LinkedIn, company websites, industry databases, and professional networks to build a verified list of contacts matching your ICP. Every email and phone number validated before it enters your pipeline.

03 📊
Lead Scoring & Enrichment

Each prospect is enriched with firmographic data — company size, revenue, growth rate — and scored by fit. Your sales team focuses on the 20% most likely to convert, not the entire list.

04 🚀
Multi-Channel Outreach

Personalised outreach launched across LinkedIn, email, and WhatsApp simultaneously. Sequences adapt based on engagement signals. Hot replies routed directly to your inbox — no manual monitoring needed.

Why Drip Wins

Broadcast Emails vs
Drip Sequences

Most businesses are still sending the same email to everyone and wondering why it doesn't work. Here's the difference that matters.

📢 Broadcast Blasts
×

Same email to everyone — whether they've been a customer for 3 years or signed up 10 minutes ago.

×

Sent once, forgotten — if they didn't open it on Tuesday, the opportunity is gone.

×

No logic based on what the recipient does — clicks, opens, replies, and silence all get the same treatment.

×

Irrelevant to most readers — a cold lead and a warm prospect receive identical messaging.

×

High unsubscribe rate — people opt out because the emails feel generic and poorly timed.

⚡ Drip Sequences
✓

Personalised to each person's actions — every lead gets a journey built around what they've actually done.

✓

Sends the right email at the right moment — triggered by behaviour, not a calendar date someone guessed at.

✓

Adapts based on opens, clicks, replies, and no-reply — the sequence forks based on what the lead actually does.

✓

Always contextually relevant — messaging matches precisely where each person is in their buying journey.

✓

High engagement, low churn — people stay subscribed because the emails feel like they were written for them.

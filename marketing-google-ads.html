FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do you manage both Google and Meta, or just one?
We manage both platforms together with a unified strategy. Budget can be split across platforms based on what's delivering the best ROAS for your business. Running both platforms together also gives us cross-channel retargeting capabilities that dramatically improve conversion rates.
What's the minimum monthly ad budget required?
We recommend a minimum of ₹30,000/month in ad spend to generate statistically meaningful data for optimisation. Below this, testing cycles are too slow — the AI can't accumulate enough signal to make confident bid and creative decisions within a reasonable timeframe.
How long before I see results?
Initial results typically appear within 2–3 weeks. Meaningful ROAS improvement usually emerges at the 4–6 week mark as the AI optimisation model accumulates enough data to make confident decisions. Campaigns continue to improve month-on-month as the system learns your audience.
Do you charge a percentage of ad spend?
No. We charge a flat monthly management fee, not a percentage of spend — so our incentive is always your ROAS, not your budget size. This means we're never tempted to recommend inflating your ad spend. Our success is measured entirely by your campaign performance.

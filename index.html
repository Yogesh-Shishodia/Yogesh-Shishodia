On-Premise

Best for: Legal, Healthcare, Defense, Govt

  • HIPAA, GDPR, CMMC, DPDP compliant

Private Cloud

Best for: Scale-ups, SaaS, Distributed Teams

Your own cloud account (AWS, Azure, GCP), your own private environment. Scalable, globally accessible, fully under your control.

  • Single-tenant — your account, your keys
  • Auto-scales to handle any load
  • HTTPS access from any browser globally
  • SOC2-eligible infrastructure
  • Pay-as-you-go — stop when not in use

Hybrid

Best for: Enterprises with mixed requirements

Sensitive data stays on-prem. Non-sensitive workflows run on cloud. Best of both — compliance without sacrificing scale.

  • Route queries by data sensitivity
  • On-prem vault + cloud compute
  • Single interface — complexity hidden
  • Customised per department or team
  • Scales as your needs evolve

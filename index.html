Deployment

Your data stays
under your control

On-premise, private cloud, or hybrid — we build for all three and recommend the right fit based on your compliance needs and team size.

🖥️

On-Premise

Best for: Legal, Healthcare, Defense, Government

The AI model, your data, and every query runs entirely on hardware you own. Nothing leaves your network — ever.

  • Data never leaves your building
  • HIPAA, GDPR, DPDP compliant
  • Works completely offline after setup
  • Full audit log on every interaction
☁️

Private Cloud

Best for: Scale-ups, SaaS, Distributed Teams

Your own cloud account (AWS, Azure, GCP). Globally accessible, infinitely scalable, and entirely under your control — no shared infrastructure.

  • Your account, your keys, your data
  • Scales automatically with your usage
  • Accessible from any browser, anywhere
  • Pay only when running
🔀

Hybrid

Best for: Enterprises with mixed requirements

Sensitive data stays on-premise. Non-sensitive workflows run on cloud. Compliance without sacrificing speed or scale.

  • Sensitive and non-sensitive data handled separately
  • Single interface — complexity stays hidden
  • Configured per department or workflow
  • Grows and adapts as your business evolves

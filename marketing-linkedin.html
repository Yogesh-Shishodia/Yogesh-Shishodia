Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Will my LinkedIn account get banned?

No. We stay within LinkedIn's rate limits, use safe automation that mimics human behaviour, and never use scrapers that violate LinkedIn's Terms of Service. We monitor account health continuously and scale slowly — your account is fully protected throughout the campaign.

How personalised are the messages really?

Every message is unique. AI reads the prospect's actual LinkedIn profile, recent posts, company announcements, and current role — and writes a message that references something specific to them. Recipients frequently reply thinking it was written by a real person who researched them. We don't send template blasts.

How long before I see results?

Most clients see first meetings booked within 2 weeks of campaign launch. Full momentum typically builds over 4–6 weeks as sequences warm up, acceptance rates optimise, and our AI learns which messaging angles convert best for your specific ICP. We share weekly performance reports throughout.

Do I need a Sales Navigator account?

It helps but isn't required. We can work with a free LinkedIn account, though Sales Navigator unlocks deeper targeting filters, higher daily outreach limits, and more precise ICP matching. We'll assess your goals and advise on whether the investment is worthwhile for your campaign — no pressure either way.

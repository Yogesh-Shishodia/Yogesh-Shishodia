Call Flow

What the AI Voice Agent does
on every single call.

From the first ring to the follow-up message — here is exactly what happens, automatically, on every inbound call.

📲
Step 1 · Call Received

AI answers within 2 rings — in a warm, natural voice

The caller hears a confident, natural greeting — not a robotic IVR menu. The AI detects whether they're speaking Hindi or English and responds in the caller's language automatically. Your brand name, tone, and agent persona are fully customised.

🎙️
Step 2 · Lead Qualification

AI asks your pre-set qualification questions naturally

The AI holds a real conversation — asking what the caller is looking for, their budget, timeline, location, and any other questions you define. Responses are understood in natural language, not just keyword-matching. The lead is scored in real time as the call progresses.

📅
Step 3 · Appointment Booking

Qualified leads get a slot booked — live, during the call

If the caller meets your qualification criteria, the AI checks your calendar in real time (Google Calendar, Calendly, or your CRM) and offers available slots. The caller confirms, and the appointment is locked — all before they hang up. No callback needed.

📝
Step 4 · CRM Update

Full call record logged to your CRM automatically

Contact details, call summary, qualification score, sentiment analysis, and appointment details are written to your CRM the moment the call ends. Your team sees a briefed, scored lead — not a name and number. No manual data entry ever.

💬
Step 5 · Follow-Up Sent

Confirmation WhatsApp/SMS sent automatically after the call

Every caller receives a personalised confirmation message via WhatsApp or SMS within seconds of hanging up — appointment details, your contact number, and a reminder link. Reduces no-shows by up to 40% compared to calls alone.

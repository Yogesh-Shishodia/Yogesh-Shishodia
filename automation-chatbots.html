The Problem

Why Generic Chatbots Fail

Rule-based chatbots looked promising. Then customers tried them. Here is exactly why they disappoint — and what we do differently.

Only handles pre-programmed FAQs

Off-the-shelf chatbots break on anything unexpected. One question outside their script and the customer hits a wall.

"I don't understand" kills conversions

Customers get dead-end responses and abandon — often at the exact moment they were ready to buy or book.

Constant manual updating

Every time a product changes, pricing updates, or a policy shifts — someone has to manually re-program the bot. It never catches up.

No memory of previous context

The customer explains their problem. The bot asks a clarifying question. The customer answers. The bot asks the exact same question again.

Our Approach

AI trained on your actual business — not generic scripts

We build chatbots trained on your real documents — product catalogue, pricing, policies, and process guides. It answers naturally, handles variations in how questions are asked, and escalates intelligently when it genuinely cannot help.

  • Retains full conversation context throughout
  • Continuous learning from unresolved queries
  • Escalates with full context — never starting blind
  • Speaks in your brand voice, not a generic bot tone

Our Process

How It Works

From brand voice training to publish-ready content — a four-step process that gets better with every piece of content we produce together.

🎨

Brand Voice Training

We analyse your existing content — website, social, emails — and define your brand's unique voice: tone, vocabulary, sentence structure, and personality. This becomes the AI's permanent brief.

📋

Content Brief

You provide the topic, product details, or campaign objective. That's it. For products: the product data. For articles: the keyword. For emails: the campaign goal. AI handles the rest.

✍️

AI Generation

AI generates the content in your exact brand voice, formatted for the specific channel and purpose — SEO metadata included where relevant. Multiple variants produced simultaneously.

✅

Review & Publish

You review and approve. AI incorporates your feedback to refine future outputs — it learns your preferences over time so the review cycle gets shorter every month.
